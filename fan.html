Ceiling Fan

Ceiling fan is Invented by Phillp Diehl in 1800s.

Fan is not giving air to us but when fan blades rotate the air around us is only giving us the air.

Ceiling Fan has some body parts like -

  1. Base plate
  2. Downrod
  3. Housing cover
  4. Motor
  5. Blade
  6. Pull switch

Fan is rotating through the magnetic feild around it.

When fan Housing cover rotate then blades rotate thats why we fell that we are geting the air .
